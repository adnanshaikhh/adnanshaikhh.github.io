Why am I blogging?

03 September 2016

I've always found that teaching someone a particular concept always reinforced my learning, so I figured writing down technical posts would be a great resource to both whoever might enjoy reading my melodramatic writing and for myself, to serve as a reference in case I forget it later. It's a bit daunting though, writing about something you feel you haven't mastered, i.e. telling someone "How you should do X". See: impostor syndrome.

I'd like to think of this process as documentation of my learning process (filled with sweet sweet gifs)
